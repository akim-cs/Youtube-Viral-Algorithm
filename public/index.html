Youtube Viral Algorithm

Andy Kim and Ryan Kim

The theme of this year's final project is "Explorable Explanation" and as I was preparing the template, I decided to build an explorable explanation myself. I've been wanting to explore approximation theory -- the math of representing complex objects with simpler, finite ones -- and this seemed like the right opportunity. Content creators on youtube have been trying to crack the viral algorithm for years, and we thought it would be interesting to explore how the algorithm works and how it can be used to create viral content.

What goes into a video blowing up? Is it the title, the thumbnail, the content, or some combination of other factors? We will explore these questions.
